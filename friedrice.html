NIGERIAN FRIED RICE

DESCRIPTION

Nigerian fried rice is a very simple yet delicious recipe. This version relies on a tons of fresh vegetables,beef liver and a little bit of oil for crisping it up.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables oil,peeled carrots,sweet peas sweet corn,and green beans.
  • 1 cup onion diced.
  • 2 scallion.
  • 1 cup beef liver cubed.
  • Half teaspoon thyme.
  • Half teaspoon curry powder.
  • Half cup chicken stock
  • 1 stock cube
  • 3 Tablespoons oil for frying
  • salt to taste
  • Quarter tsp cayenne pepper

STEPS

  1. Add the parboiled Rice to the stock and cook until the water is dried up.
  2. Preheat the oil in a pan,throw in the onions and fry for a minute or two,add the scallions, mixed vegetables,thyme,curry powder,salt,and stock cube.
  3. Throw in the beef liver and rice.Stir-fry for about 3 to 5 minutes.
