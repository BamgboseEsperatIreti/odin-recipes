JOLLOF RICE

DESCRIPTION

This traditional Nigerian dish brings out the best possible flavour of rice and chicken.Chicken is first sauteed on the stove to produce the best aromatic base for the rice.For an extra special and authentic touch,serve jollof rice with fried plantains.

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh pepper
  • Vegatable oil
  • Onion
  • Seasoning cubes and salts
  • curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Ripe Plantain

STEPS

  1. Wash rice and parboiled,drain and set it aside.
  2. Pour vegetable oil in a pan and heat up,add chopped onion and pepper and tomato paste .Stir and fry until well fried.Add enough water that can cook the parboiled rice.
  3. Add seasoning cubes and salt to taste.Add curry powder and thyme as desired. Allow to boil a little then add the parboiled rice.Cover and allow to cook until the rice is done.
  4. Fry plantain and add salt if desired.
  5. Your JOLLOF RICE WITH PLANTAIN is ready!ENJOY THE DELICIOUS MEAL.
