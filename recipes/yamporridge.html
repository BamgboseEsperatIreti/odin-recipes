YAM PORRIDGE

DESCRIPTION

Yam porridge,also known as Asaro,is a very delicious yam recipe though its eaten by most tribes in Nigeria however,it is more common in the yoruba speaking parts of Nigeria. Its also easy to make.

INGREDIENTS

  • puna yam
  • blended pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • palm oil
  • seasoning

STEPS

  1. Its best to use the freshest palm oil you can possibly get because stale plam oil will ruin the taste of the pottage.
  2. Its best to use as little seasoning as possible because the yam itself is sweet so excessive seasoning will overshadow the sweet taste of the pottage.
  3. Add a little more water if necessary but dont overdo it.
